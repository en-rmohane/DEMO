Admission Confirmation

Pay admission fees and complete registration formalities

Requirements

Eligibility Criteria

B.Tech Programs

  • 10+2 with Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics
  • Minimum 45% aggregate marks (40% for reserved category)
  • Valid JEE Main score (for admission through counseling)
  • Direct admission based on 12th marks (subject to availability)
  • Age limit: Minimum 17 years as on 31st December

MBA Program

  • Bachelor's degree in any discipline
  • Minimum 50% aggregate marks (45% for reserved category)
  • Valid CAT/MAT/CMAT/XAT/ATMA score
  • Direct admission based on graduation marks
  • Final year students can apply provisionally

M.Tech Programs

  • B.Tech/B.E. in relevant discipline
  • Minimum 55% aggregate marks (50% for reserved category)
  • Valid GATE score preferred
  • Non-GATE candidates can apply
  • Industry experience considered for working professionals

EWS Scholarship

Upto 100%

For students from economically weaker sections as per government norms.

Eligibility Criteria:
  • Annual family income below ₹8 lakhs
