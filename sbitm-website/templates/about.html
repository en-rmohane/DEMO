Affiliated to Rajiv Gandhi Proudyogiki Vishwavidyalaya (RGPV), Bhopal and approved by AICTE, New Delhi, the institute has consistently maintained excellence in academics, research, and placements.

Our Philosophy

Vision & Mission

Our Vision

To be a premier institution of technical and management education that produces globally competent professionals, fosters innovation, and contributes to societal development through quality education, research, and ethical values.

Our Mission

  • To provide quality education through innovative teaching-learning processes and state-of-the-art infrastructure
  • To promote research, innovation, and entrepreneurship among students and faculty
  • To develop industry-institute collaboration for skill enhancement and employability
  • To inculcate ethical values, social responsibility, and environmental consciousness
