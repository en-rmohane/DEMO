Requirements

Eligibility Criteria

B.Tech Programs

  • 10+2 with Physics, Chemistry & Mathematics
  • Minimum 45% aggregate marks (40% for SC/ST)
  • Valid JEE Main/State Entrance Exam score
  • Age limit: 25 years (relaxation as per norms)
  • Direct admission through MP-DTE counseling

MBA Program

  • Bachelor's degree in any discipline
  • Minimum 50% aggregate marks (45% for SC/ST)
  • Valid CAT/MAT/CMAT/ATMA score
  • Group Discussion & Personal Interview
  • Work experience preferred but not mandatory

BBA Program

  • 10+2 in any stream from recognized board
  • Minimum 45% aggregate marks (40% for SC/ST)
  • No age limit for admission
  • Direct admission based on merit
  • Personal interview for final selection

Financial Support

Scholarships & Financial Aid

Merit Scholarship

Up to 100% tuition fee waiver for top rankers

  • JEE Main Rank < 10,000
  • Class 12th > 90%
  • Renewable annually based on performance
  • Available for all programs

SC/ST Scholarship

Government scholarships for reserved categories

  • Full tuition fee waiver
  • ₹1,000 monthly maintenance allowance
  • ₹2,000 annual book grant
  • Hostel fee concession

Girls Scholarship

Special scholarship for female students

  • 25% tuition fee waiver
  • All engineering and management programs
  • Merit-based renewal each year
  • Additional hostel fee discount

Sports Scholarship

For students with sports achievements

  • State/National level players
  • Up to 50% fee waiver
  • Sports facility access
  • Training and coaching support
