The placement process involves student registration, company registration, pre-placement training, campus recruitment drives, and offer letters. Companies conduct pre-placement talks, written tests, group discussions, and interviews on campus.

For 2024, the highest package was ₹22 LPA and the average package was ₹6.5 LPA. Packages vary based on the company, job profile, and student's skills and performance.

Companies can register through the online registration form above or contact the placement cell directly via email at placement@sbitm.edu.in or phone at +91-83495-12345.

We provide comprehensive training including aptitude tests, technical skills development, soft skills training, mock interviews, group discussions, resume building, and corporate etiquette sessions.

Students must have minimum 60% aggregate throughout academics, no active backlogs, and must complete all placement training sessions. Specific companies may have additional criteria.
