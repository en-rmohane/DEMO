Help

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I apply for admission?

You can apply for admission through our online portal or by visiting the campus. The online application process includes filling the form, uploading documents, and paying the application fee.

What are the hostel facilities available?

We provide separate hostels for boys and girls with facilities including Wi-Fi, mess, gym, TV room, and 24x7 security. Rooms are available on single/double sharing basis.

Is there any scholarship available?

Yes, we offer various scholarships including merit-based, need-based, and special category scholarships. Details are available on our website or admission office.

Still Have Questions?
